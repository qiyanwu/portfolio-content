Visit US

Tour & Tasting Experience

Enjoy an intimate 90-minute Tour & Tasting experience that explores the history of the property, its unique terroir, and its superlative wines. Learn the fascinating stories of one of California's earliest wine estates as you stroll the property and finish with a seated tasting. This is a group experience, there will be other groups with your group.

Porch Tasting

Join us for a rare opportunity to enjoy a private seated tasting on the porch of the historic Manor House. During your tasting you will enjoy a wonderful mixture of appellated Estate wines, as you are transported back to the time when the property was a fashionable country resort.
