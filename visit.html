Tour & Tasting Experience

France is the top destination when it comes to wine tastings and French wineries are those offering the most diverse experiences. Taste some of the most amazing wine in the world in singular ways and take advantage of the gorgeous landscape of the French countryside or the coast.Enjoy an intimate 90-minute Tour & Tasting experience that explores the history of the property, its unique terroir, and its superlative wines. Learn the fascinating stories of one of California's earliest wine estates as you stroll the property and finish with a seated tasting. This is a group experience, there will be other groups with your group.

Porch Tasting

Wine tasting is a subjective experience on a couple of different levels. For one, tasting the wine itself is a very personal experience. Individual taste preferences and experiences can vary depending on the style of wine(s) you end up sampling. In addition, the setting itself can have a dramatic impact on how your mind interprets the overall experience. Join us for a rare opportunity to enjoy a private seated tasting on the porch of the historic Manor House. During your tasting you will enjoy a wonderful mixture of appellated Estate wines, as you are transported back to the time when the property was a fashionable country resort.
